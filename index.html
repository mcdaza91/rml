How it works

Reach My Leads instantly converts all incoming web leads into live phone calls.

1. A lead completes and submits one of your online forms

Reach My Leads can receive data from any source. Connect your CRM, website forms, Facebook campaigns, landing pages, and more.

2. Reach My Leads generates a call to your sales team

Once the lead is submitted Reach My Leads will automatically trigger a call to your business in seconds. If they don't pick up, we can try them again or try a different number with our Round Robin Routing feature.

3. Press 1! Your sales rep and lead are now connected

Reach My Leads plays a custom recording with the lead's info and prompts you to press 1 to connect. Your hot new lead instantly gets a call from your business number before they can change their mind.

features

Lead response management has never been easier.

Reach My Leads enables you to automate the communication between your sales team and your leads, minimizing the need for manual outreach.

Call Recording

Listen to how your sales team is handling your hard-earned leads. Record all calls to help you monitor and train your team better. Enjoy hearing your leads’ reaction when they get a call in seconds.

Custom Caller ID

Choose the phone number you want your leads to see and can call you back if they miss the call. Setup outgoing Caller ID with your business phone number or your salesperson's cell phone.

Round Robin Routing

Reach My Leads will call the next sales rep listed if the first one doesn’t answer.
Setup up to 5 different reps/numbers or call the same number multiple times.

Lead Tracking Reports

Track every lead, conversion rates, length of calls and much more. See which lead sources are performing better. Download, share or print any report.
